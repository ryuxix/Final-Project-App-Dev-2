Routine 1: Quick Warm-Up (15–20 Minutes)

Aiming

  • Range Practice (10 Minutes):
    • Static Bots (3 Minutes): Shoot 50 easy bots while standing still.
    • Flick Shots (3 Minutes): Use medium bots and practice flicking to their heads.
    • Tracking (4 Minutes): Enable moving bots and track them with your crosshair before shooting.
  • Deathmatch (10 Minutes): Focus on crosshair placement, quick peeks, and burst firing.

Movement

Spend 5 minutes in a custom game practicing strafing while shooting at walls or targets. Focus on counter-strafing to stop quickly for accurate shots.

Routine 2: Intermediate Training (30–45 Minutes)

Aiming

  • Aimlabs Playlist (10–15 Minutes): Use tasks like Gridshot and RA Head Click for flicking and precision.
  • Range Practice (10 Minutes):
    • Use the Guardian on "Eliminate 50" with strafing enabled.
    • Practice spray transfers on drones outside the range using the Vandal or Phantom.
  • Deathmatch (10–15 Minutes): Alternate between aggressive playstyles and defensive playstyles.

Movement

Practice bunny hopping and air-strafing in a custom game. Use maps like Icebox or Split to practice skill jumps that provide tactical advantages.

Routine 3: Advanced Session (1 Hour+)

Aiming

  • Aimlabs/Aim Trainer (15–20 Minutes): Advanced tasks like Microshot or Switchtrack for micro-adjustments.
  • Range Drills (15 Minutes):
    • Practice headshots with the Sheriff on hard bots to refine precision.
    • Use the Phantom/Vandal for burst fire drills, focusing on recoil control.
  • Deathmatch Variations (20 Minutes):
    • Play one game focusing only on headshots.
    • Play another game emphasizing movement—use counter-strafing and jiggle peeking to outplay opponents.

Movement

Create a custom game to practice jump peeking, parkour routes, and tactical repositioning.

Routine 4: Combined Mechanics/What the PRO do Routine

Aiming + Movement Sync

  • Horizontal Line Aiming Drill (5 Minutes): Strafe left/right while keeping your crosshair steady along a horizontal line.
  • Corner Tracking Drill (5 Minutes): Move around corners while keeping your crosshair aimed at potential enemy positions.
  • Custom Game Movement Practice (10–15 Minutes):
    • Practice strafing, jiggle peeking, jump peeking, and counter-strafing against bots in a custom match.
  • Deathmatch with Movement Focus (15–20 Minutes):
    • Incorporate all movement techniques into Deathmatch games: jiggle peeks, bunny hopping, etc.
