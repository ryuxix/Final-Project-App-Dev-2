Fortnite Training Routines

Sharpen your aim, building, and movement skills with these tailored routines!To save routine copy the text before saving it!

Routine 1: Quick Warm-Up (15–20 Minutes)

Aiming

  • Aim Training (10 Minutes):
    • Use a Creative aim map (e.g., Raider464’s Mechanics Map).
    • Practice flicking, tracking, and crosshair placement on targets.
  • Aim Duels (5 Minutes):
    • Engage bots or friends in close-range 1v1 box fights.

Movement

Freebuild in Creative for 5 minutes. Practice basic building patterns (90s, ramps, walls) and focus on smooth movement and clean edits.

Routine 2: Intermediate Training (30–45 Minutes)

Aiming

  • Aim Trainer Maps (10–15 Minutes):
    • Use scenarios like Gridshot, Tile Frenzy, or AR Tracking in Aimlabs/Kovaaks or in-game maps.
  • Edit Courses (10 Minutes):
    • Run through edit courses to improve speed and accuracy.
  • Aim Duels (10–15 Minutes):
    • Alternate between aggressive and defensive playstyles in box fights or aim duel maps.

Movement & Building

  • Movement Drills (5 Minutes):
    • Practice sliding, mantling, and double movement in Creative maps.
  • Piece Control Practice (5 Minutes):
    • Rehearse taking control of walls, cones, and ramps against bots or friends.

Routine 3: Advanced Session (1 Hour+)

Aiming

  • Advanced Aim Trainer (15–20 Minutes):
    • Use high-difficulty scenarios for micro-adjustments and rapid target switching.
  • Range Drills (15 Minutes):
    • Practice long-range AR tracking and close-range shotgun flicks in custom maps.
  • Aim Duels (20 Minutes):
    • Play extended box fight sessions, focusing on headshots and rapid edits.

Movement & Building

  • Freebuilding with Edits (10 Minutes):
    • Practice fast-paced freebuilding, including high ground retakes and complex edit chains.
  • Movement Integration (10 Minutes):
    • Combine sliding, mantling, and peeking in Creative maps; focus on right-hand peeks for tactical advantage.

Routine 4: Combined Mechanics/Pro Routine

Aiming + Movement Sync

  • Horizontal Line Tracking Drill (5 Minutes):
    • Strafe left/right while keeping your crosshair steady on a horizontal line or wall.
  • Corner Peeking Drill (5 Minutes):
    • Practice moving around corners and keeping your crosshair aimed at likely enemy positions, using right-hand peeks.
  • Custom Game Movement Practice (10–15 Minutes):
    • Practice advanced movement (jiggle peeking, jump peeking, sliding) and combine with rapid building/editing in Creative maps.
  • Box Fight Deathmatches (15–20 Minutes):
    • Play box fight matches focusing on combining all movement, building, and aiming techniques under pressure.
