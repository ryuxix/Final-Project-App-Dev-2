CS2 Training Routines

Master your aiming, movement, and utility skills with these Counter-Strike 2 routines!

Routine 1: Quick Warm-Up (15–20 Minutes)

Aiming

  • Bot Aim Practice (10 Minutes):
    • Start a custom game with bots on "Aim Botz" or "training_aim_csgo2" maps.
    • Focus on headshots with the AK-47 or M4A4.
    • Practice flick shots and tracking by moving your crosshair between bots quickly.
  • Deathmatch (5–10 Minutes):
    • Join a Deathmatch server and focus on crosshair placement and quick reaction times.
    • Use only headshots to improve precision.

Movement

Spend 5 minutes practicing strafing, counter-strafing, and peeking corners in a custom game. Use walls or bots as targets while practicing movement-based shooting.

Routine 2: Intermediate Training (30–40 Minutes)

Aiming

  • Aim Trainer Maps (15 Minutes):
    • Use maps like "Yprac Aim Trainer" or "training_aim_csgo2" to practice flicks, tracking, and precision.
    • Set up scenarios for long-range duels to improve distance accuracy.
  • Deathmatch (10–15 Minutes):
    • Play one Deathmatch game focusing on burst fire control with rifles.
    • Switch to pistols for the second half to improve close-range accuracy.
  • Recoil Control Practice (5 Minutes):
    • Shoot at a wall in a custom game to practice spray patterns for the AK-47, M4A4, and MP9.
    • Focus on controlling sprays while strafing left and right.

Movement

Create a custom game to practice bunny hopping, jump peeking, and jiggle peeking. Use maps like "Yprac Movement Trainer" for advanced movement drills.

Utility Practice

  • Nade Practice (10 Minutes):
    • Load into maps like Mirage or Inferno and practice throwing key smokes, flashes, and molotovs for common choke points.
    • Use workshop maps like "Yprac Nade Trainer" to refine grenade lineups.

Routine 3: Advanced Session (1 Hour+)

Aiming

  • Aim Trainer Maps (20 Minutes):
    • Use advanced training maps like "Aim Botz" or "Fast Aim Reflex Training."
    • Focus on flick shots, reaction speed, and multi-target engagements.
  • Shooting Drills (10 Minutes):
    • Shoot at moving targets in a custom game with bots set to high difficulty.
    • Practice one-tap headshots with the Deagle or USP-S to improve precision under pressure.
  • Deathmatch Variations (20 Minutes):
    • Play one Deathmatch focusing only on headshots with rifles.
    • Play another focusing on movement-based shooting (e.g., jiggle peeks).

Movement

Create a custom map session to practice advanced movement techniques like strafing while shooting, bunny hopping through choke points, and jump peeking corners. Use maps like "KZ Climb" for parkour-style movement challenges.

Utility Practice

Create a private server to practice advanced grenade lineups. Focus on learning key smokes for retakes and executes on maps like Mirage, Inferno, or Overpass. Spend time practicing pop flashes to blind opponents effectively during pushes.

Routine 4: Combined Mechanics Routine

Aiming + Movement Sync

  • Horizontal Line Aiming Drill (5 Minutes): Strafe left/right while keeping your crosshair steady along a horizontal line.
  • Corner Tracking Drill (5 Minutes): Move around corners while keeping your crosshair aimed at potential enemy positions.
  • Custom Game Movement Practice (10–15 Minutes):
    • Practice strafing, jiggle peeking, jump peeking, and counter-strafing against bots in a custom match.
  • Deathmatch with Movement Focus (15–20 Minutes):
    • Incorporate all movement techniques into Deathmatch games: jiggle peeks, bunny hopping, etc.

Utility Practice

  • Advanced Grenade Lineups (10 Minutes):
    • Practice smokes for map control (e.g., Mirage window smoke, Inferno CT smoke).
    • Learn pop flashes to support team pushes or blind enemies holding angles.
